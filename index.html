What is Water Optics?

At Water Optics, we're building smart, user-friendly kiosks that make water quality testing simple and accessible for everyone. Our mission is to help individuals understand what’s in their water — from heavy metals to hardness levels — and provide clear, actionable insights to improve it. Whether you're using well water, city water, or just want peace of mind, our kiosks bring lab-style analysis to your fingertips, right in your local store or community space.

Next

How Will We Achieve this?

We’re achieving this by combining cutting-edge sensor technology, computer vision, and automated strip analysis in a compact, standalone kiosk. Users simply insert a water sample and test strip, and our system captures the results, interprets them with precision, and presents easy-to-understand feedback on-site. With a focus on affordability, accessibility, and accuracy, we’re making advanced water testing available to everyone — no lab required.
