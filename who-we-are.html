Meet The Team

Who we are

We are a team of senior engineering students from North Carolina State University, united by a shared passion for innovation and public health. With diverse backgrounds across electrical, mechanical, and chemical engineering, we’ve come together to develop accessible water testing solutions that empower individuals to understand and improve their water quality.

  • Learn More

Our Mission

At Water Optics, our mission is to empower people with clear, accessible information about the water they drink. Through innovative water quality testing kiosks, we make it easy for anyone to understand what’s in their water — and what to do about it.
