Frequently Asked Questions

What does our kiosk test for?

Our kiosk analyzes a wide range of water quality parameters including pH, hardness, heavy metals (like lead and copper), chlorine, nitrate/nitrite levels, and more.

How does the kiosk work?

It’s simple: insert your water sample into the kiosk, and our system automatically captures and analyzes the color results. Within moments, you’ll see a personalized breakdown of your water quality on our website.

Is this as accurate as a lab test?

While not a replacement for certified lab analysis, our kiosks use carefully calibrated sensors and standardized strips to provide highly reliable readings that are more than sufficient for everyday health and safety awareness.

Will I understand the results?

Absolutely. We provide color-coded indicators and simple descriptions (like "OK", "High", or "Needs Attention") for each parameter, along with recommendations for improving your water quality if needed.
